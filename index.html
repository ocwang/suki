It's not catering

Suki Sushi brings a pop-up restaurant to your event.

We believe in doing things differently.

Whatever you want to eat, whenever you want to eat it. Suki Sushi offers fresh, made-to-order dining options for parties looking for something more refined than traditional catering.

It's like having a full-kitchen restaurant in your house.

How it Works:

Decide what you'd like to eat at your event

Contact us and we'll find a time to meet and dicuss what types of food you'd like to have at your event.

We'll take care of prepping all the ingredients

We'll buy all the ingredients and prep them right before the event. Suki Sushi only uses fresh, high quality products.

On the day of the event, we'll set up our signature made-to-order kitchen

Immediately, we'll start off by making classic favorites so hungry guests don't have to wait on ordering.

Relax and enjoy. We'll take care of keeping everybody full.

Whatever you'd like to eat, whenever you want to eat it. Let us know and we'll make it right in front of you.
